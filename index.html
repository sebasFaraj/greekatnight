Who we are

We're a Gyro Food Truck located at Kirkwood Street in Bloomington Indiana. We are dedicated to being a reliable and affordable late night snack for every Bloomington Resident. Make sure to give us a visit at any time from 10pm to 4am

Our Mission

To provide every customer with an elite service and a delicious gyro that puts the cherry on top on a every fun night out

Our Vision

To be the most reliable, affordable, and elite dining option for the nightlife of Bloomington
